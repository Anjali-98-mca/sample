TKM COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING,KOLLAM

The Thangal Kunju Musaliar College of Engineering, commonly known as TKMCE,is the first government aided engineering institution in the Indian state of Kerala. It is located in the Karikode neighborhood, 6 kilometres (3.7 mi) from the city of Kollam in southern Kerala.
